How It Runs

From call to current, in five steps.

No surprise callouts, no vague estimates. This is the same sequence for a single socket or a full commercial rewire.

01 / CALL

You call or message us

Tell us what's happening — a live phone line usually gets you a same-day slot for anything urgent.

02 / INSPECT

We inspect on site

A licensed electrician checks the wiring, load, and panel condition before recommending anything.

03 / QUOTE

You get a written quote

Fixed price, materials listed, no changes once work starts unless you ask for something extra.

04 / INSTALL

We do the work

Clean cable runs, proper earthing, and a tidy site when we're done — not just a working switch.

05 / TEST

Test, walkthrough & handover

Every circuit is load-tested and shown to you before we consider the job finished.
